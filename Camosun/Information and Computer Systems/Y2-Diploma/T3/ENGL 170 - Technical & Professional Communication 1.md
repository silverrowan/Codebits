# ENGL 170 - Technical & Professional Communication 1
Credits: 3 Total Hours: 45 This course focuses on writing for the workplace, including writing formats, structure, style, and document design with a comprehensive review of grammar, punctuation and usage. Students will write workplace correspondence, short reports, instructions and job-search formats in a clear, concise, purposeful, unambiguous and grammatically correct way.
