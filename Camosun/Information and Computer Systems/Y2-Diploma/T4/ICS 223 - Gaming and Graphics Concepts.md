# ICS 223 - Gaming and Graphics Concepts

Credits: 3 Total Hours: 75 Restricted to students in Information Computer Systems Technology Programs
Are you curious about how interactive experiences like games are created? You will explore the intersection of creativity and technology, learning key concepts in game design, event-driven programming, user interaction, and immersive systems. Using industry-standard tools, gain hands-on experience creating functional interactive projects that are applicable across various fields, including software development, simulation, and interactive media. Whether applied to game design or other tech solutions, these skills provide a solid foundation for building innovative digital applications in a variety of industries.

Prerequisites: One of:    C+ in COMP 139 ,     C+ in ICS 124 
And One of:     C in ENGR 155 ,     C in ICS 125
