# ICS 280 - Capstone Project Preparation
> [!info] 
>  **Credits::** 1.5
**Total Hours::** 15
**Restricted::** Information and Computer Systems, Interactive Media Developer

Kickstart your Capstone Project journey in this dynamic, fast-paced course designed for students in both the Information and Computer Systems and Interactive Media Developer programs. In this prep course, faculty will assign you to a team based on your unique strengths, ensuring that you collaborate with a well-rounded group. You’ll meet industry sponsors, explore real-world project opportunities, and work with your team to select a project aligned to your group’s strengths. Focused on teamwork, communication, and project planning, this course sets the foundation for a successful Capstone Project, preparing you for the hands-on work to come.

> [!attention] 
> **Prerequisites::** [[ICS 212 - Database Systems Management]] C, [[ICS 214 - C++ Programming]] C, [[ICS 226 - Network and Server-Side Programming]] C, [[ICS 228 - Computer Ethics and Security]] C
**Co-requisites::** [[ICS 200 - Special Topics in Computing]] C, [[ICS 215 - Systems Analysis and Design]] C, [[ICS 221 - Web Services]] C, [[ICS 223 - Gaming and Graphics Concepts]] C, [[ICS 224 - Mobile App Development]] C
