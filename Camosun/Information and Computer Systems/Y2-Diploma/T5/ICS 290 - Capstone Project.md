# ICS 290 - Capstone Project
> [!info] 
>  **Credits::** 6
**Total Hours::** 300
**Restricted::** Information and Computer Systems, Interactive Media Developer

Tackle real-world software challenges in a collaborative, project-driven experience that emphasizes innovation and problem solving. With your team, you will design, implement, and manage software solutions while refining technical documentation and communication skills, all in alignment with industry standards and security practices. With a focus on strategic planning, time management, and conflict resolution, this experience prepares you to navigate complex projects and excel in professional technology roles and ready to embrace a role in industry.
